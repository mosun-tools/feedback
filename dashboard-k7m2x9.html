    Advanced — listener score weights iControls how raw form answers become the 0–100 Listener score. Each weight is that metric's share of the blend (normalized, so relative size is what matters). Penalty is the max points subtracted when every listener flags weak parts. Defaults are sensible — only touch these if a metric matters more or less to you than the blend assumes.
